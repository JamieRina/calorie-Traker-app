BiteBalance Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, password hash, and authentication/session records.
  • Profile and health-related data: age or date of birth, sex, height, weight, target weight, activity level, goals, dietary preferences, calories, nutrients, meals, progress entries, and workouts.
  • App data: favourites, recipes, reminder settings, sync records, and support/security logs.

How We Use Data

We use this data to create your account, secure your session, calculate goals, track nutrition and progress, send password reset emails, and provide core app functionality.

Third-Party Services

The app may use USDA FoodData Central, Open Food Facts, OpenAI recipe parsing when configured, SMTP email delivery, PostgreSQL, Redis, and backend hosting infrastructure. This repository does not include ads, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or payment SDKs.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in Settings. Deletion removes your account and associated profile, goal, meal, progress, workout, reminder, token, sync, and user-owned recipe records from the app database.

Contact

Contact us through the support email listed with the app distribution platform or production website.
